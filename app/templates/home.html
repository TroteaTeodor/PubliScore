How It Works

  1. Search for an address or location in Antwerp
  2. Our algorithm calculates accessibility based on:
    • Number of public transport stops nearby
    • Distance to the nearest stops
    • Types of transport available (bus, tram, train, etc.)
  3. Get a score from 0-10 indicating how well-connected the location is
  4. Use this information to make informed housing decisions

About The Data

PubliScore uses open data from OpenStreetMap for Antwerp to calculate accessibility scores. The data includes the locations of all public transport stops, stations, and other points of interest that affect accessibility.

Our algorithm considers multiple factors to provide a comprehensive accessibility score that reflects real-world transportation options.
