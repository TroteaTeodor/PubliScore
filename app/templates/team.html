Project Background

PubliScore was developed as part of a hackathon project focused on creating tools to improve urban mobility and accessibility in Antwerp. Our goal was to create a simple yet effective way to evaluate locations based on their public transport connections.

Using open data from OpenStreetMap, we developed an algorithm that can provide an accessibility score for any location in Antwerp, helping residents make more informed housing decisions that support sustainable transportation choices.
